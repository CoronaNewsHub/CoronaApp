Excess mortality caused by the novel coronavirus (COVID-19)

The actual number of deaths from the novel coronavirus in the Netherlands is higher than the reported numbers published by RIVMNational Institute for Public Health and the Environment . However, the numbers for the distribution of mortality by, for example, age, gender and region do correspond. This is revealed by an analysis carried out by RIVM and Statistics Netherlands (CBS) based on monitoring of mortality rates in the Netherlands.

RIVM launches study on coronavirus herd immunity

RIVMNational Institute for Public Health and the Environment has launched a large-scale study to investigate how many people have antibodies for the novel coronavirus (COVID-19). Invitations to take part in the study will be sent to 6,000 people from all over the country and from all age groups. In the coming months, RIVM will measure antibodies in the blood in several rounds of research.
